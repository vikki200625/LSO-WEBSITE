Frequently Asked Questions

Your top questions answered.

Do I need an account to use these services?

Yes. Create a free account to donate, book a blood donation appointment, or save wellness content. It helps us personalize your experience and keep your history safe.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use secure best practices and never store sensitive card details on our servers. Your donations are processed safely.

How do I book a blood donation appointment?

Go to Blood Donation, choose a slot, and submit your details. You’ll get a confirmation and reminders as the date approaches.

Can I see where my donation goes?

Yes—visit the Donations and Articles sections to view stories and impact updates. Your dashboard also shows your personal history.

Is the yoga content suitable for beginners?

Yes. Start with the beginner routines. Each session includes clear guidance, modifications, and safety tips.
